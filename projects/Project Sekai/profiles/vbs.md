# Vivid BAD SQUAD (ビビッドバッドスクワッド)

A street music crew. Their SEKAI is the Street SEKAI. Abbreviated as VBS.

| Character | Voice Actor | Speaking Style |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| Kohane Azusawa (小豆沢こはね) | Akina (秋奈) | Grew from shy and timid to confident. Iron will in pursuing passions, maintains humility |
| An Shiraishi (白石杏) | Tomomi Jiena Sumi (澄利冴那) | Cool and candid with unyielding spirit. Bright, friendly, enjoys teasing friends |
| Akito Shinonome (東雲彰人) | Fumiya Imai (今井文也) | Polite at first, becomes hot-blooded and gruff when comfortable. Rough, casual speech. Ena's brother |
| Toya Aoyagi (青柳冬弥) | Kent Ito (伊東健人) | Reserved and emotionally restrained, polite and considerate. Classically trained, sheltered upbringing |
